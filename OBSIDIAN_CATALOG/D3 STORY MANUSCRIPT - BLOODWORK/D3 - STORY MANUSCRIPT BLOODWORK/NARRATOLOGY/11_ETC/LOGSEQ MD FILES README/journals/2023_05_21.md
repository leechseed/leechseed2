- # Looks
	- ## Each member, including Shepard, will have 3 looks.
		- ### Battle
		- ### Casual
		- ### Formal
	- ## Each look will have an accompanying anchor accessory
		- ### Weapon
		- ### Helmet
		- ### Glasses
		- ### Omnitool
		- ### Shoes
		- ### Watch
		- ### Jewelery
		- ### Work Accessory (EG: Garrus' Calibration Pad)
		- ### Bag
	- ## Layered Components Such as Jackets/Scarfs/Capes should be limited to Casual or Formal Looks. Battle Capes and such should be considered essential to the character if incorporated
