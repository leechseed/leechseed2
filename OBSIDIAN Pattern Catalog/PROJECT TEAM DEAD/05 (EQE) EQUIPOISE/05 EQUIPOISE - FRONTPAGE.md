> **05 EQUIPOISE** - Equipoise refers to a state of balance or equilibrium within a narrative, maintaining harmony among characters, plot lines, themes, or perspectives. This state of balance may be disrupted by the introduction of conflict, a crucial element that challenges the characters, induces change, and propels the narrative towards its climax and resolution. 
> 
> This interplay of balance and conflict serves as the dynamic engine of storytelling, engaging readers and shaping the narrative's course.
